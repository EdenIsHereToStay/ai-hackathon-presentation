Project 1: 3D Super Tic Tac Toe

3D Super Tic Tac Toe with AI and AR Capabilities

Problem: Traditional Tic Tac Toe games are limited in complexity and engagement.

Solution: A 3D Super Tic Tac Toe game with multiplayer support, AI opponents, and AR capabilities.

Why: Achievable within the timeframe, blend of fun and technology.

Challenges: Balancing simplicity and innovation, integrating AR features.

Project 2: AiSchool Ecosystem

AiSchool Ecosystem Project

Problem: Traditional education systems often fail to cater to individual learning needs.

Solution: An open-source, AI-driven platform for personalized K-12 education.

Why: Potential to revolutionize education, making learning more personalized and engaging.

Challenges: Developing a robust and adaptive AI, creating engaging content for K-12 students.

Project 3: TheRadio.Ai

TheRadio.Ai

Status: Over 100 songs on Lake County Underground Radio, featuring over two dozen artists.

Vision: Revolutionize the music industry with AI-managed radio stations.

Why: Merges passion for music and AI, significant impact on the entertainment industry.

Challenges: Improving UI/UX, scaling the backend, integrating AI for station management.

Project 4: CORE.ASi

CORE.ASi

Vision: An open-source ASi core for building AI models offline.

First Step: Develop an application to connect AI models through intermediate architecture.

Why: Groundbreaking potential to democratize AI technology.

Challenges: Developing a robust application to seamlessly connect different AI models.

Conclusion

Each project presents unique opportunities and challenges.
